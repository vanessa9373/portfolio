About Me

Turning business requirements into production-grade cloud infrastructure

I build cloud infrastructure that doesn't break at 2 AM. As a Solutions Architect, Cloud Engineer, DevOps Engineer, and Site Reliability Engineer, I design multi-cloud environments across AWS, Azure, and GCP that are secure, cost-efficient, and self-healing — from reusable Terraform modules to Kubernetes platforms to fully automated CI/CD pipelines.

I believe the best way to demonstrate skill is to ship. I've built 18 production-grade infrastructure labs, an 18-lab Kubernetes Mastery Guide covering beginner to expert, and a 40-module multi-cloud Terraform library — all open source on GitHub. Every project reflects how I work: methodical, well-documented, and built to scale.

I'm completing a B.S. in Computer Science at Western Governors University (May 2026) and hold an AWS Solutions Architect Associate, CKA, and Linux Essentials certification. My earlier Bachelor's in Business Management gives me something many engineers lack — the ability to translate business requirements into technical decisions stakeholders actually understand.
